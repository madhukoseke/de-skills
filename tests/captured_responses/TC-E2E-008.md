## Summary
The PR review should follow a structured checklist with explicit PASS/FAIL/WARN/N-A statuses, then conclude with a risk assessment and merge recommendation.

## Decision
Run a PR_REVIEW checklist across BigQuery and Airflow changes, assign status per item, compute a risk score, and issue `REQUEST_CHANGES` when blocking FAIL findings exist.

## Rationale
A checklist-driven review prevents critical misses in reliability, cost, and data quality. Risk scoring standardizes reviewer decisions and approval thresholds.

## Trade-offs
| Option | Pros | Cons | When to Use |
|--------|------|------|-------------|
| Structured checklist review | Repeatable quality gate | Slightly longer review cycle | Production DE PRs |
| Ad-hoc review | Faster for trivial changes | Inconsistent risk coverage | Tiny non-critical docs changes |
