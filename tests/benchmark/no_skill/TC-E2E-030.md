## Summary
Handle the post-release incident with root cause analysis and mitigation.

## Decision
Build incident timeline, execute remediation, and document follow-up in postmortem.

## Rationale
A disciplined incident process reduces blast radius and recurrence.

## Trade-offs
| Option | Pros | Cons | When to Use |
|--------|------|------|-------------|
| Rollback mitigation | Fast stabilization | May delay feature rollout | Severe regressions |
| Fix-forward only | Maintains release | Higher ongoing risk | Minor incidents |

## Next Steps
1. Capture blast radius and timeline.
2. Apply rollback or mitigation.
3. Track remediation in postmortem.
