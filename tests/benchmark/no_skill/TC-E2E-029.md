## Summary
Production PRs should use pass/fail checklisting and explicit risk review.

## Decision
Score risk, record pass and fail items, and issue approve or request_changes based on unresolved concerns.

## Rationale
Structured review improves safety for production changes.

## Trade-offs
| Option | Pros | Cons | When to Use |
|--------|------|------|-------------|
| Strict checklist | Better consistency | Slower review | High-risk PRs |
| Lightweight review | Faster cycle | Hidden issues | Low-risk updates |
