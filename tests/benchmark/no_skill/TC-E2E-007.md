## Summary
A streaming design should prioritize ordering, resilience, and sink throughput.

## Decision
Use a broker topic with partitioning by user key and a stream processor to load warehouse tables with at-least-once or exactly-once strategy as needed.

## Rationale
Per-key ordering supports user-level correctness, and processing guarantees depend on business tolerance for duplicates.

## Trade-offs
| Option | Pros | Cons | When to Use |
|--------|------|------|-------------|
| Exactly-once | Strong correctness | More operational overhead | Critical financial events |
| At-least-once | Better throughput | Requires dedup logic | High-volume telemetry |
