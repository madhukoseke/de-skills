## Summary
Diagnose the incident through a structured root cause workflow.

## Decision
Start with triage, identify likely root cause, apply remediation, and record follow-up actions.

## Rationale
A repeatable incident process shortens recovery time and reduces recurrence.

## Trade-offs
| Option | Pros | Cons | When to Use |
|--------|------|------|-------------|
| Structured incident flow | Faster stabilization | Process overhead | Customer-impacting failures |
| Ad-hoc debugging | Flexible | Inconsistent outcomes | Low-severity issues |

## Next Steps

2. Isolate probable root cause and fix forward.
3. Document remediation and postmortem actions.
