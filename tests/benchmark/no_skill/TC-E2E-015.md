## Summary
A star schema with one fact and multiple dimensions is appropriate for this analytics domain.

## Decision
Design a central fact table for transaction grain and supporting dimension tables with surrogate keys for customers, products, and date.

## Rationale
Star schema and kimball modeling patterns support performant BI queries and clear semantic boundaries between measures and attributes.

## Trade-offs
| Option | Pros | Cons | When to Use |
|--------|------|------|-------------|
| Star schema | Fast analytics queries | More joins than OBT | Standard reporting marts |
| OBT | Simpler query surface | Hard to maintain history | Small fixed datasets |
