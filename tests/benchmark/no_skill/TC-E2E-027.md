## Summary
The issue is likely a throughput bottleneck increasing latency during peak load.

## Decision
Measure bottleneck stages and tune concurrency/parallelism with queue backpressure controls.

## Rationale
Capacity imbalance is a common cause of peak-time degradation.

## Trade-offs
| Option | Pros | Cons | When to Use |
|--------|------|------|-------------|
| Capacity tuning | Better throughput | Higher compute cost | Peak traffic systems |
| Do nothing | No immediate cost | Continued latency | Non-critical flows |
