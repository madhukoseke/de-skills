## Summary
Renaming a shared field is a breaking change and needs migration.

## Decision
Use deprecation phase, migration path, and rollback fallback.

## Rationale
Staged change reduces production risk for downstream dependencies.

## Trade-offs
| Option | Pros | Cons | When to Use |
|--------|------|------|-------------|
| Staged migration | Lower disruption | Longer transition | Shared contracts |
| Immediate rename | Quick cleanup | High break risk | Isolated systems |

## Next Steps
1. Publish breaking change notice.
2. Implement migration.
3. Keep rollback option ready.
