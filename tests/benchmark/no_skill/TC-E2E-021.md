## Summary
A bridge pattern is suitable for many-to-many relationships.

## Decision
Use a factless bridge table to connect dimensions at clear grain.

## Rationale
Bridge structures avoid duplication and keep m2m relationships explicit.

## Trade-offs
| Option | Pros | Cons | When to Use |
|--------|------|------|-------------|
| Bridge model | Handles many-to-many cleanly | More joins | Promotion mappings |
| Flattened model | Simple query | Cardinality errors | Small prototypes |
