## Summary
Use canary release and rollback preparation for safer deployment.

## Decision
Roll out in phases, monitor key SLO metrics, and keep runbook rollback steps ready.

## Rationale
Canary limits blast radius and supports controlled release confidence.

## Trade-offs
| Option | Pros | Cons | When to Use |
|--------|------|------|-------------|
| Canary deploy | Safer releases | Slower promotion | Critical systems |
| Full release | Faster delivery | Larger risk | Low-impact changes |
