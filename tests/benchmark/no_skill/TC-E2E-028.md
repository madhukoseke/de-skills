## Summary
Adopt a layered test strategy for confidence across development and release.

## Decision
Use unit test, integration, and e2e suites with reusable fixtures and assertion checks.

## Rationale
Layered tests improve defect detection while preserving deployment speed.

## Trade-offs
| Option | Pros | Cons | When to Use |
|--------|------|------|-------------|
| Full test pyramid | Strong quality control | Maintenance overhead | Production pipelines |
| Minimal tests | Faster setup | Higher regression risk | Prototypes |
