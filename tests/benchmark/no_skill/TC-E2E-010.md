## Summary
Handle the request in two phases: pipeline architecture then warehouse model design.

## Decision
First define ingestion architecture, then produce `create table` DDL with partition strategy and a cost estimate.

## Rationale
Separating architecture from modeling reduces ambiguity and improves implementation quality.

## Trade-offs
| Option | Pros | Cons | When to Use |
|--------|------|------|-------------|
| Sequential design | Clearer ownership | More steps upfront | End-to-end planning |
| Combined design | Faster draft | Easier to miss constraints | Very small projects |
